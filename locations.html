Where can I go to ride?


The riding options in and around Harrisonburg are endless! If you are interested in mountain biking - We have Hillandale Park, the George Washington National Forest is just a thirty minute drive away, and the Massanutten Western Slope. For our road riders it only takes a short mile to get out of the city and hit awesome country roads!
