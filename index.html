Who are the Harrisonburg Cyclists?


The Harrisonburg Cyclists are a cohort of riders, who all vary in skill, endurance, and experience. Whether you enjoy mountain biking or road riding - In the Shenendoah Valley, there are endless places to go!


Feel free to check out some of our weekly group rides, local races, and places to around the area to bike. If you are interested in being added to our email chain please fill out the form under the Contact tab!
